\section{Methods}

\subsection{Study Design}

This study evaluates the robustness of PET/CT radiomics features under simulated noise perturbation conditions using open cancer imaging datasets.

\subsection{Data Sources}

Candidate datasets include TCIA PET/CT collections and the AutoPET dataset.

\subsection{Image Preprocessing}

Planned preprocessing includes voxel harmonization, metadata normalization, and perturbation simulation.

\subsection{Noise Perturbation Framework}

Gaussian and Poisson perturbation strategies are evaluated to simulate low-count PET acquisition conditions.

\subsection{Radiomics Extraction}

Radiomics features are extracted using PyRadiomics.

\subsection{Robustness Metrics}

Feature robustness is evaluated using ICC, coefficient of variation, and agreement analyses.
