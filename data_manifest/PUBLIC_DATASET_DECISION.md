## Required before benchmark execution

1. Confirm dataset license and access terms.
2. Record citation and access date.
3. Document image format, voxel spacing, and preprocessing assumptions.
4. Define segmentation source or segmentation workflow.
5. Record radiomics software and extraction settings.

## Boundary

Dataset selection and perturbation protocol must be frozen before expanding robustness claims.
