# Open Data Sources

## TCIA FDG-PET-CT-Lesions

- Source: The Cancer Imaging Archive (TCIA)
- Modality: FDG PET/CT
- Focus: Whole-body lesion imaging
- Candidate uses:
  - radiomics stability;
  - segmentation robustness;
  - low-count PET simulation.

## AutoPET

- Source: AutoPET Challenge
- Modality: PET/CT
- Focus: Automated lesion segmentation
- Candidate uses:
  - segmentation benchmarking;
  - perturbation testing;
  - denoising evaluation.

## TCIA LUNG-PET-CT-DX

- Source: TCIA
- Modality: PET/CT
- Focus: Lung cancer imaging
- Candidate uses:
  - oncology radiomics;
  - feature repeatability;
  - lesion-noise sensitivity.

## TCIA PSMA-PET-CT-Lesions

- Source: TCIA
- Modality: PSMA PET/CT
- Focus: Prostate cancer imaging
- Candidate uses:
  - cross-modality robustness;
  - PET biomarker stability.

## Repository data policy

Do not commit raw imaging archives.

Allowed:

- manifests;
- checksums;
- derived tables;
- scripts;
- metadata;
- compressed figure outputs.
